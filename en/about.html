About Cercis

Our Story, Our Passion

Our Journey

Founded in Madrid with a vision to transform spaces through the art of floral design.

At Cercis - Taller de Arte Vegetal, we believe in the power of nature to create unforgettable experiences. Our journey began with a simple passion for combining artistry with botanical elements, evolving into a respected name in Madrid's event decoration scene.

Every arrangement we create tells a unique story, reflecting both our commitment to excellence and our clients' vision.

Artistry

Every creation is a unique piece of art, carefully crafted to inspire and delight.

Passion

Our love for natural beauty drives us to create extraordinary experiences.

Excellence

We maintain the highest standards in every aspect of our work.

Our Team

Led by passionate artists and floral designers, our team brings together years of experience in event decoration and botanical artistry. We work collaboratively to bring your vision to life, ensuring every detail is perfect.
